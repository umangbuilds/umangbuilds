## What I'm Building

**[MoltPe](https://moltpe.com)** — payment infrastructure for AI agents. A wallet for your agent, not access to yours.

Agents spend, collect, and settle in USDC across Base, Polygon, and Tempo. 9 MCP tools, x402 V2 micropayments, and the first agent-native collections engine.

→ [Live product](https://moltpe.com) · [Open source reference](https://github.com/umangbuilds/moltpe-agent-payments) · [Research](https://moltpe.com/research)
